The Problem

Deciding where to go out is a gamble.

📸

Stale Photos

Venue profiles show their best Friday night — not what it looks like right now at 9pm.

📋

Old Reviews

A 3-star Google review from 6 months ago tells you nothing about tonight's atmosphere.

🤷

No Real Signal

You only find out a venue was dead when you're already standing in it.

"Instagram Live is retention — keeping your existing followers engaged. VibeCheck is acquisition — converting undecided people into walk-ins tonight."

The Solution

Real-time venue discovery,
powered by the people inside.

1
Promoters live stream from inside the venue
Shot on mobile, broadcast directly through the app — shows what's actually happening right now.
2
Streams appear on the venue's story feed, ranked by freshness
Live now → Fresh tonight → More venues. Activity-based ranking surfaces the hottest spots first.
3
Viewers browse, watch, and decide — no account needed
Open the app, see what's popping nearby, make a decision with real confidence.

Powered by LiveKit
Under the Hood

Built to Scale

📱 Mobile App

Expo + React Native. Viewers watch live streams. Promoters broadcast directly from their phone camera using the LiveKit SDK.

🌐 Web App

Next.js 15. Full venue management — create, edit, manage promoters. Promoters can also broadcast live from the web broadcast page.

⚡ API

Express.js on Node. REST endpoints with JWT auth. PostgreSQL via Prisma ORM. Manages LiveKit room lifecycle and issues access tokens per broadcast.

📡 Live Streaming

LiveKit handles real-time room creation, WebRTC media transport, and viewer delivery — ultra-low latency with no custom media infrastructure.

Business Model

Revenue tied directly to results.

Core

Per-Venue Invoicing

Venues are billed based on foot traffic attribution — how many viewers indicated intent to visit, then arrived.

Growth

Aggregated Analytics

Premium upsell: venue owners get aggregated data on attendance trends, stream performance, and peak activity windows.

Pipeline

Vibe Score

A rolling aggregate vibe score, fed by viewer reactions. High-scoring venues surface higher in discovery — incentivising quality content.

2-sided
Marketplace

Venues are incentivised to only post when it's actually good — a dead room after a promising stream destroys trust. The platform self-regulates for quality.
